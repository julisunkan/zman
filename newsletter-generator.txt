AI Newsletter Generator
=======================

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Craft stunning, send-ready email newsletters in moments — no copywriting skills needed. AI Newsletter Generator lets you describe your topic, pick your audience and voice, and watch as advanced AI builds a complete, beautifully formatted HTML email from scratch. Every newsletter ships with a bold header, well-organised body sections, a compelling call-to-action, and a clean footer — all wrapped in rock-solid inline styles that render perfectly across every major email client and device.

FEATURES
--------
- Instant AI-written newsletter from any topic or industry
- Precision audience targeting (e.g. small business owners, tech enthusiasts, HR teams)
- Five distinct writing voices: Professional, Casual, Inspiring, Friendly, Educational
- Flexible layout with 2 to 5 custom content sections
- Personalised call-to-action button with editable label text
- 100% self-contained HTML using fully inlined CSS — no external dependencies
- Real-time rendered preview directly inside the browser
- Raw HTML source panel for quick copy-paste into any platform
- Instant one-click download as a standalone HTML file
- Built-in history library retaining your 20 latest newsletters
- Optimised subject line crafted to maximise open rates
- Drop-in compatible with Mailchimp, Outlook, Gmail, SendGrid, and more
