AI Activity Book Generator
==========================

DESCRIPTION
-----------
An AI-powered activity book creator that generates complete, themed puzzle and trivia books for kids and learners of all ages. Choose a theme (Space, Animals, History, Ocean…), difficulty level, and age group, and the tool instantly assembles four distinct activities — a word search, a Sudoku puzzle, a crossword with clues, and a trivia quiz — all woven around your chosen topic. Download the finished book as an interactive HTML file or play it directly in the browser.

FEATURES
--------
- Theme-based activity book generation (any topic)
- Difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
- Age groups: Kids 6–8, Kids 9–12, Teens, Adults
- Word search puzzle with hidden theme-related words
- Sudoku puzzle (9×9 grid with appropriate difficulty)
- Crossword puzzle with across/down clues
- Trivia quiz with multiple-choice questions
- Interactive in-browser play for all four activities
- Word search click-to-reveal word highlighting
- Crossword interactive grid with clue highlighting
- Trivia answer reveal per question
- Downloadable interactive HTML activity book
- Auto-saved book history (up to 20 recent books)
- Color-coded activity tabs (Word Search, Sudoku, Crossword, Trivia)
