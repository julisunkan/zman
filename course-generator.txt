AI Course Generator
===================

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Transform any subject into a fully structured, professional course in seconds. Course Generator harnesses the power of cutting-edge large language models to craft comprehensive learning programs complete with rich lesson content, interactive quizzes, and hands-on assignments — no teaching experience required. Whether you're an educator, corporate trainer, or lifelong learner, create publish-ready courses and download them as beautifully formatted PDF or Word documents with a single clic


FEATURES
- One-click course creation from any topic or niche
- Choose between 2 and 50 learning modules per course
- Three expertise tiers: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced
- Generate courses in dozens of languages worldwide
- Control lesson depth with word counts from 100 to 1,500 per module
- Built-in multiple-choice assessments (up to 15 questions per module)
- Actionable project assignments that reinforce every lesson
- Live progress indicator with real-time content streaming
- Download the entire course or cherry-pick individual modules as PDF
- Export to Microsoft Word (DOCX) for easy editing and branding
- Automatic course library saves your 20 most recent creations
- Interactive in-app reader with instant quiz grading and score tracking
- Visual difficulty indicators for at-a-glance course management
